Robert “Rob” Kerbs

Gemologist · Gem Cutter · Educator

Robert “Rob” Kerbs is a gemologist, gem cutter, and computer scientist living in Southern California.

His work and interests have crossed several fields, including computer science, higher education, gemology, faceting, science education, and music.

He has long been drawn to disciplines that reward precision, patience, and careful judgment — from technical education to the study and cutting of gemstones.
